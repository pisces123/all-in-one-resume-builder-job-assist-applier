# Design Brief

This project should not default to generic SaaS, startup, AI-tool, or stock "modern landing page" aesthetics.

Act as a senior web designer, brand strategist, copywriter, and front-end developer.

## Product

- Business name: All in One Resume
- Offer: Resume builder, job saver, job tracker, ATS-readiness guidance, tailored resume drafts, cover letters, and interview/job-search support.
- Target audience: Individual job seekers who need a practical place to organize applications and prepare better materials.
- Primary goal: Get the visitor into the workspace and make the Chrome saver feel useful.
- Brand personality: Calm, focused, premium, practical, and human.
- Main CTA: Open Workspace.

## Avoid

- Purple-blue gradients
- Glassmorphism cards
- Floating dashboards
- Random glowing blobs
- Generic feature grids
- Startup-style hero sections
- Vague corporate copy
- Buzzwords like "unlock," "supercharge," "streamline," and "seamless"

## Create

- A distinct visual concept
- Strong typography
- Specific human copy
- Purposeful spacing
- A memorable hero section
- Layout choices that fit job seekers
- A clear conversion path

Before major landing-page builds, propose 3 creative directions, choose the strongest one, and build from that.
